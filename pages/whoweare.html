Who We Are The Students of SPS

We are students drawn together by our love of physics (and love of pizza!). We come from many unique backrounds and represent all of the different programs that the physics department has to offer. Though most of us are physics majors, students from other majors taking physics classes, students considering switching to a physics major, and others who just find the subject interesting are all welcome at our meetings.
